Maps, Tools, & Resources

Utah Household and Jobs Forecast Viewer

In alignment with the 4 year update cycle for long range transportation plans, Utah's MPOs and UDOT produce a year-by-year forecast for where Utahn's will live and work. The Utah Household and Jobs Forecast web map shows these forecasts for the states 9000+ traffic analysis zones (TAZs) and for city area approximations.

2023 Utah Moves Transportation Study

Utah's transportation agencies conduct a survey of household travel patterns & decision-making in order to inform travel forecasting and impact analyses for future projects. Use the Data Explorer to view summary-level results or read the project report or complete documentation for the core travel survey and supplemental survey.

Statewide Traffic Volume Forecast

One of our most popular web maps, this statewide map shows historic, current estimates, and forecasted future average daily traffic volumes (AADT) for major roads and highways (collectors and above) across Utah in map and graph formats.
