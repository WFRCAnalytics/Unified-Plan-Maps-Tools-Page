Maps, Tools, & Resources

Utah Household and Jobs Forecast Viewer

Where will Utahns live and work in the future?

In alignment with the 4-year update cycle for long range transportation plans, Utah's MPOs and UDOT produce a year-by-year forecast for Utah households and employment. This statewide web map shows these forecasts for the state's 9000+ traffic analysis zones (TAZs) and for city area approximations.

2023 Utah Moves Transportation Study

How and why do residents of Utahn travel?

Utah's transportation agencies conducted a survey of household travel patterns & decision-making in order to inform travel forecasting and impact analyses for future projects. Use the Data Explorer to view summary-level results or read the project report or complete documentation for the core travel survey and supplemental survey.

Statewide Traffic Volume Forecast

What will traffic conditions look like in the future?

This statewide web map shows historic, current estimates, and forecasted future average daily traffic volumes (AADT) for major roads and highways (collectors and above) across Utah in map and graph formats. Datasets used in this application are available for download via the 'About' panel.
